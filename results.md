
## Research Results

Tested 5 water sources in Ho Chi Minh City (March 2026):

| Source | TDS @25°C (ppm) | QCVN Status |
|--------|----------------|-------------|
| Tap water | 103 | ✓ Pass |
| Home RO filter | 105 | ✓ Pass |
| Aquafina | 1 | ✓ Pass |
| Saltwater (test) | 983 | ✓ Pass |
| Natural pond | 171 | ✓ Pass |

Key finding: Home RO filter TDS (105 ppm) nearly equal to 
tap water (103 ppm), suggesting filter effectiveness had degraded.
Temperature compensation (ISO 7888) changed saltwater reading 
from 1081 ppm to 983 ppm — critical for accurate comparison.
